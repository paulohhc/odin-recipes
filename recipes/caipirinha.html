Caipirinha

Description

Caipirinha is a Brazilian cocktail made with lemon and "cachaça", a typical spirit in that country.

Recipe retrieved from https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/20210/caipirinha/ .

Ingredients

  • ½ lime, quartered;
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar;
  • 2 ½ fluid ounces cachaça (Brazilian rum);
  • 1 cup ice cubes.

Directions

  1. Squeeze and drop lime wedges into a large rocks glass. Add sugar and muddle until juices release;
  2. Pour in cachaça and ice. Stir well.
